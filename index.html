Tapestry Commissions, tufted rugs and wall hangings

Jeni Ross is a UK artist who designs and makes contemporary handwoven tapestries.
Commissioned interior artworks include woven tapestries, tufted textile wall hangings and rugs and her work can be seen throughout the UK including Oxford University, hospitals, theatres, schools, museums, a major scientific centre and the Government Art Collection. Private commissions include one off tapestries and tufted rugs.

Working with yarns offers richness and depth of colour and allows Jeni's strong colourful designs to bring texture and content to both private homes and public buildings.  The medium of textile transforms the design with an additional language of intensity and unique depth of colour. It is this translation of image into a tactile structure that makes it so effective in an architectural setting.

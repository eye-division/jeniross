Biography
COMMISSIONS/COLLECTIONS include:
  • 2007 - 12 Commission for Founders Library, New College, Oxford. "Dance to the Music of Time" Suite of 6 Woven tapestries,  which were exhibited at the Ashmolean Museum in September/October 2012. The tapestries will be installed at New College in 2015.
  •  “BLUE” Commissioned installation New Ashgate Gallery
  • Series of tufted textile artworks for Belfast HSS Trust Childrens’ Treatment Centres and Shannon Clinic, Belfast.
  • Tapestry for House of Lords Offices. Government Art Collection.
  • Tapestry, Carpet & etched glass for Norwich Castle Museum.
  • Tufted Textile Wall Pieces for Salford Hospital, Manchester & St Luke's Hospital South Tees NHS  Trust                       
  • Two Woven Tapestries for Strode Theatre, Street, Somerset.
  • Tufted Textile Wall Piece for Oldham NHS Trust. 
  • 3 Tapestries for Sheerness Public Library, Isle of Sheppey, Kent.                              
  • Tapestries and Carpet for Main Entrance, Central Science Laboratories, Ryedale, York for MAFF. Public Arts/RMJM London Ltd.
  • Tapestry, Mid-Kent Oncology Centre, Maidstone, Powell Moya
  • Tapestry for Lyceum Theatre,Sheffield; JG Graves Trust.
  • Set of 13 large woven tapestry cushions for Lambeth Palace Chapel, London.
